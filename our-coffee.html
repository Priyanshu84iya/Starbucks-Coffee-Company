Our Coffee

From bean to cup, we're committed to the finest coffee experience

Our Coffee Story

For more than five decades, Starbucks has been sourcing and roasting exceptional coffee. Today, we're the premier roaster of specialty coffee in the world. And with every cup, we strive to bring both our heritage and an exceptional experience to life.

Our Roasts

Each coffee requires a slightly different roast to reach its peak of aroma, acidity, body and flavor

Blonde Roast

Lightly roasted coffee that's soft, mellow and flavorful. Easy-drinking on its own and fantastic with milk, sugar or flavored with vanilla, caramel or hazelnut.

Medium Roast

Smooth and balanced, these coffees are perfect as your everyday cup. Look to medium roasts to deliver the perfect balance of aroma, acidity, and body that defines exceptional coffee.

Dark Roast

Fuller-bodied and bold, these coffees feature robust and intense flavors. The beans' sugars begin to emerge during the roasting process to complement the coffee's natural flavors.

Coffee Origins

Brewing Methods

Discover different ways to brew the perfect cup at home

Pour Over

A manual brewing method that allows you to control every variable for a perfect cup.

French Press

Immersion brewing that produces a full-bodied, rich cup of coffee.

Cold Brew

Smooth, refreshing coffee that's slowly steeped in cool water.

Espresso

Concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee.

Ethical Sourcing

We believe in sourcing coffee in a way that creates a better future for farmers and a more stable climate for the planet. That's why 99% of our coffee is ethically sourced through our Coffee and Farmer Equity (C.A.F.E.) Practices.

Environmental Goals

Our aspiration to become resource positive by 2030

Water Conservation

50% reduction target

Conserve or replenish 50% of water used for direct operations and coffee production by investing in community water projects.

Waste Reduction

50% reduction target

Cut waste in half by shifting away from single-use packaging, investing in innovative reusable cup programs, and developing recyclable solutions.

Carbon Neutral

50% reduction target

Reduce carbon emissions by 50% through renewable energy, reforestation, and sustainable coffee farming practices.
